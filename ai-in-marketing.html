Course

AI in Marketing

Hands-on application of artificial intelligence to content creation, customer insights, and AI-powered marketing strategy.

Overview

Course Description

This course prepares students to use artificial intelligence (AI) in modern marketing practice. As organizations increasingly rely on AI tools to generate content, analyze customer data, personalize experiences, and automate decisions, marketers must be able to effectively work with and manage AI systems.

The course takes a hands-on, applied approach to AI in marketing. Students learn how to use AI tools to design marketing campaigns, generate and evaluate content, analyze customer insights, and build AI-assisted customer experiences. Rather than focusing on programming, the course emphasizes practical skills, strategic thinking, and critical evaluation of AI outputs.

Throughout the semester, students work on real-world marketing tasks and complete a semester-long group project where they design an AI-powered marketing strategy for a business.

Objectives

Course Learning Objectives

  • Use AI tools to generate and refine marketing content (ads, emails, social media)
  • Apply prompt design techniques to improve AI-generated outputs
  • Analyze customer insights using AI-assisted methods
  • Evaluate AI-generated content for effectiveness, clarity, and trust
  • Design AI-supported marketing strategies across the customer journey
  • Develop and present a complete AI-driven marketing plan
  • Critically assess the strengths and limitations of AI in marketing decisions
Topics

What Students Learn

AI and Consumer Response

Students evaluate how customers respond to AI-generated outputs, including persuasion, trust, and perceived control.

Prompt Design as a Marketing Skill

Students learn how to design and refine prompts to improve the quality and usefulness of AI-generated outputs.

AI for Customer Insights

Students analyze customer reviews and text data using AI to identify patterns, needs, and actionable insights.

AI Content and Brand Voice

Students develop marketing content aligned with brand voice across platforms using AI tools.

Advertising and Campaign Design

Students generate, compare, and refine AI-assisted advertising content and campaign ideas.

Customer Experience and Chatbots

Students design AI-supported customer journeys and chatbot interactions.

AI Analytics and Optimization

Students evaluate AI outputs and design testing strategies to improve marketing performance.

Pedagogy

Applied Learning Approach

This course is highly hands-on and application-focused. Each session combines short lectures, live demonstrations, in-class exercises, and group-based activities that simulate real marketing tasks.

Students actively use AI tools to create, test, and refine marketing outputs. A semester-long group project allows students to develop an AI-powered marketing strategy in a realistic business context.

Assessment

Course Requirements & Assessment

  • Midterm Exam (AI-based, applied tasks): 200 points
  • Final Exam (AI-based, comprehensive): 200 points
  • Individual Assignments (AI tasks): 200 points
  • Group Project Milestones: 200 points
  • Final Group Project: 150 points
  • Participation & In-Class Activities: 50 points
Expectations

Student Expectations

  • Bring a laptop for in-class AI activities
  • Actively engage in hands-on exercises
  • Experiment with new AI tools
  • Think critically about AI outputs
  • Work collaboratively in teams
  • Clearly explain and justify AI use
