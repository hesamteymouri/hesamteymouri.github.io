Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I study how emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence shape customer experiences. My research provides managerial implications built on deep theoretical foundations and rigorous methods.

My research focuses on Consumer–Technology Interactions (AI Systems, Service Technologies, Digital Platforms), Retail and Service Experience Design, Customer Dissatisfaction and Well-Being.
