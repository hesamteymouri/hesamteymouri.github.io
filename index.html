Research

My research examines customer-based marketing strategies by leveraging customer data to generate meaningful managerial insights. I study how firms can better understand customer experiences, judgments, and behaviors to inform marketing decisions that create value for both firms and customers.

Research Project

Retail Donation Design and Prosocial Behavior

I examine how retail donation design influences charitable giving, engagement, and related consumer responses, showing how marketplace design can encourage prosocial action.

Research Project

Investigating how agentic AI systems that replace human judgment in service decisions affect consumers’ feelings of control and autonomy, and how these perceptions influence marketplace responses.

Preparing for submission: Journal of Marketing Research (FT 50, ABDC: A*)
