Digital Marketing

Strategy, analytics, and customer journey design in modern digital environments.

Course Description

This course introduces students to digital marketing as a strategic discipline. Students learn how organizations build digital presence, attract customers, and create value across multiple online touchpoints.

The course emphasizes practical marketing decision-making and explores how digital tools support customer acquisition, engagement, and retention.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the strategic role of digital marketing in modern organizations
  • Analyze the digital customer journey across multiple platforms
  • Evaluate website performance and digital presence
  • Understand SEO, SEM, social media marketing, and email marketing
  • Use digital analytics to support marketing decisions
  • Develop an integrated digital marketing strategy

What Students Learn

SEO

Search engine optimization and organic visibility strategies.

Search Advertising

Principles of search engine marketing and campaign performance.

Website Strategy

Website structure, landing pages, and conversion design.

Social Media Marketing

Campaign design and engagement strategies across platforms.

Email Marketing

Customer communication strategies and campaign effectiveness.

Web Analytics

Measuring digital performance and marketing KPIs.

Applied Learning

Students work on practical assignments that connect digital strategy to real marketing decisions. Projects involve analyzing website performance, designing digital campaigns, and developing integrated digital marketing strategies.

Students also work with real digital analytics data to answer marketing questions and generate strategic insights.
