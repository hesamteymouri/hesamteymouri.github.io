Digital Marketing

Course Page

Course Description

This course introduces students to the strategic foundations of digital marketing. Students learn how organizations attract, engage, and retain customers using digital channels such as search engines, social media, and online advertising platforms.

Topics Covered

  • Search Engine Marketing (SEO & PPC)
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Digital Advertising Strategy
  • Content Marketing
  • Customer Analytics
  • Conversion Funnels and Landing Pages

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how digital marketing fits into overall marketing strategy
  • Analyze online customer behavior and digital metrics
  • Design digital campaigns using modern marketing tools
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of digital marketing initiatives
