Course

AI in Marketing

Understanding how artificial intelligence is transforming marketing strategy, decision-making, and customer experience.

Overview

Course Description

This course examines the growing role of artificial intelligence in marketing. Students learn how AI technologies are used to support decision-making, personalize customer experiences, and automate marketing processes across digital platforms.

The course focuses on both managerial and behavioral perspectives, helping students understand how AI changes how firms interact with customers and how customers respond to automated systems.

Objectives

Course Objectives

  • Understand the role of AI in modern marketing strategy
  • Evaluate how AI influences customer decision-making and experience
  • Analyze AI applications in personalization, automation, and targeting
  • Understand ethical and managerial implications of AI use
  • Use AI tools to support marketing analysis and content development
  • Develop AI-informed marketing strategies
Topics

What Students Learn

AI and Consumer Behavior

Students examine how consumers perceive and respond to AI systems, including trust, fairness, control, and decision-making processes.

AI in Customer Experience

Students explore how AI shapes service interactions through chatbots, recommendation systems, and automated decision-making.

Personalization and Recommendation Systems

Students learn how AI enables personalized marketing and how firms tailor content, products, and communication to individual customers.

Generative AI in Marketing

Students use tools such as ChatGPT to generate marketing content, analyze customer data, and support creative decision-making.

AI and Marketing Strategy

Students examine how firms integrate AI into marketing strategy, including targeting, positioning, and competitive decision-making.

Ethics and AI

Students evaluate ethical issues related to AI, including bias, transparency, fairness, and the role of human oversight in automated decisions.

Pedagogy

Applied Learning Approach

Students engage in hands-on projects using AI tools to analyze customer data, generate marketing content, and evaluate AI-driven decisions. The course emphasizes applied learning where students critically assess how AI is used in real marketing contexts.

Through case studies and projects, students learn to connect AI capabilities with marketing strategy and customer experience design.
