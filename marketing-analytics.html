Marketing Analytics

Data-driven marketing decision making using statistical analysis, programming tools, and real-world datasets.

Course Description

This course introduces students to marketing analytics as a quantitative approach to understanding customers, evaluating marketing performance, and supporting strategic business decisions. Students learn how organizations use data, statistical models, and analytical tools to analyze markets and improve marketing outcomes.

The course combines marketing theory with hands-on data analysis. Students work with real datasets and use analytical tools such as R, Python, Excel, and AI-assisted analytics tools to explore customer behavior, estimate demand, evaluate marketing interventions, and generate data-driven insights for marketing decision making.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the role of data and analytics in modern marketing decision making
  • Prepare and analyze marketing datasets to identify patterns and trends
  • Apply statistical and quantitative models to marketing problems
  • Use analytical tools such as R, Python, Excel, and AI-assisted analytics platforms
  • Interpret analytical results to inform marketing strategy and business decisions
  • Communicate data-driven insights through reports and visualizations

What Students Learn

Marketing Data Analysis

Students work with real marketing datasets to explore patterns in customer behavior, demand, and marketing performance.

Demand Modeling

Students learn how statistical models can be used to estimate demand and understand how marketing actions influence customer decisions.

Statistical Analysis for Marketing

Students apply quantitative methods to evaluate marketing campaigns and measure the effectiveness of marketing interventions.

Programming for Analytics

Students gain exposure to tools such as R or Python for data preparation, visualization, and modeling.

Data Visualization

Students learn how to communicate analytical insights through charts, dashboards, and analytical reports.

AI-Assisted Analytics

Students learn how modern AI tools can support data exploration, interpretation, and the generation of marketing insights.

Applied Learning

Students collaborate in teams on hands-on analytics projects that simulate real marketing decision environments. Using statistical models, programming tools, and AI-assisted analysis, students analyze marketing datasets, evaluate demand patterns, and generate strategic insights.

Assignments emphasize practical problem solving, teamwork, and the ability to translate analytical results into clear and actionable marketing recommendations.
